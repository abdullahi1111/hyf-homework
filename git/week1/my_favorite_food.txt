1-Instant Pot Beef Chili
A piping-hot pot of chili – no matter what the regional style – is a great remedy for a tiring day. This instant pot beef chili is super simple to make, and you can add or subtract any ingredients to your liking, including modifying the spice level. 
Not only is it flavorful and low-maintenance, but it also makes a large serving size,
 so you can enjoy leftovers that will be just as good the next day.
