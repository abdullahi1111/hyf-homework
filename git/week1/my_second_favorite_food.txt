2-Chicken Pot Pie
This hearty classic is bursting with chicken, potatoes, peas, carrots and onions tossed in a creamy filling. 
You can save some time during prep by using rotisserie chicken and mixing the shredded pieces right into the gravy. And instead of rolling out pastry dough, use store-bought puff pastry sheets or a ready-made pie crust. 
This delicious homemade chicken pot pie recipe uses a bag of frozen hash browns for added ease.easy
